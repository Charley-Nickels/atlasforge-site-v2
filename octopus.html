Octopus in Action

A cozy, character-driven city-builder starring Mayor Octavius — a stylish, monocled octopus in a top hat — and a town full of quirky animal citizens. Built as both a standalone experience and a proving ground for Atlas-V and AtlasStudio.

Official OIA brand: orange octopus, monocle, top hat — playful but clean.

The World

Your town begins small — a city hall, a few homes, a handful of citizens — but expands through tasks, civic decisions, and delightful interactions. Every resident has personality, routines, and needs.

As the Mayor (with Octavius guiding the tone), you balance comfort, community, and growth, all within a cozy simulation framework.

Cozy City-Building

Construct buildings, manage civic tasks, and nurture a growing town, all represented in a clean pixel-art aesthetic.

Character-Driven Simulation

Each citizen has personality. Some chatty. Some shy. Some causing problems. Some helping fix them.

AtlasForge Testbed

OIA serves as a live showcase for Atlas-V modules, AtlasStudio workflows, and the overall AtlasForge tool philosophy.
