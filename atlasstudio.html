AtlasStudio Workstation

Zero-code surface for building logic, screens, and flows.

AtlasStudio Workstation (short: AtlasStudio) is a simulated tri-panel UI for managing tasks, decisions, and snapshots. Atlas-V runs internally to keep routes, guardrails, and memory aligned.
