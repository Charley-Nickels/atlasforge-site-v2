Stack-integrated design

Built on the same logic as your tools.

Octopus in Action isn’t a separate “game project” — it’s the flagship simulation that proves out the AtlasStudio + Atlas-V mental model.

  • AtlasStudio: used to shape tasks, loops, and patch notes for the harbor.
  • Atlas-V: routes decisions, explains outcomes, and keeps the world safe from dead-end states.
  • Shared language: the same concepts that tune a sim can later tune a dashboard or operations flow.

Why start with a harbor?

Harbors are naturally full of queues, schedules, and routes. Perfect for testing decisions and timelines:

  • Who gets dock access when space is tight?
  • Which tasks reach the player first each morning?
  • How do civic choices ripple into work and home life?
