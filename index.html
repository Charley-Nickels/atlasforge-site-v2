How it works

Structured guidance for any project

Atlas Engine sets the pattern, Atlas-V applies it, and Atlas-V Lite brings the same flow to GPT experiences.

Phases and runs

Projects break into phases (big chunks) and runs (focused tasks) so teams can move methodically.

Guidance and check-ins

Atlas-V issues step-by-step execution prompts for your tools, then asks for quick run reports to keep progress on track.
