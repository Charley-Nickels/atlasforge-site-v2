How it works

Guided phases and runs

Atlas Engine structures projects into phases and runs. Atlas-V delivers step-by-step prompts, and the Playground shows how to feed back snapshots.

Phases

Big chunks of a project organized by the Engine.

Runs

Bite-sized tasks with clear goals.

Execution prompts

Guided steps tailored to the tools you already use.

Run reports

Quick updates that let Atlas-V adjust and keep the plan on track.

Studio surface

Visualizes tasks, decisions, and previews without shipping code.
