Audio division

S/K/B

Signals · Kinetics · Bloom. The AtlasForge audio lab for stems, soundscapes, and AI-driven voices designed for worlds built on the stack.

What S/K/B focuses on

S/K/B is where music and sound design experiments live. It’s a place to sketch how the audio side of the Atlas stack might feel before anything is wired into real tools.

  • Voices — murmur personas and AI vocal shapes.
  • Signals — generative stems and loop beds.
  • Echoes — ambient soundscapes and OST sketches.
S/K/B Audio Lab

Integration with Atlas-V

In future, S/K/B output could route through Atlas-V: adapting stems, voice selection, and ambience based on in-world context.

For now, it’s a quiet observatory: concepts and motifs waiting to be wired in.
