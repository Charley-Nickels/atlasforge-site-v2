Methodology

The governance loop

Brief, generate, validate, and ship. Every step is wrapped in glow cues so teams know which system they’re touching.

  • Briefs originate in AtlasStudio using canon-safe templates.
  • Atlas-V validates prompts and files against canon.
  • Decisions are logged in the console with ready-to-ship diffs.
  • Releases hit OIA, AtlasWave, or other experiences with the same glow language.

Story of the glow

We use glow cues for clarity, not noise. Halo bars mark transitions, orbs spotlight key tiles, and hero frames keep everything contained.
