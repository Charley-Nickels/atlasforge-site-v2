Hybrid game & tools studio

Build worlds and workflows on the same stack.

AtlasForge Interactive is a small studio building a shared stack for games, internal tools, and intelligent systems. AtlasStudio, Atlas-V, Octopus in Action, and SKB all live on the same mental model: decisions, timelines, and stories.

The Atlas stack

Three pillars, one mental model.

Every project in AtlasForge uses the same idea: treat your world like a routing problem between people, tools, and time. The art and stakes change, the structure stays familiar.

AtlasStudio

A three-panel workstation for shaping content, behavior, and flows. Built to feel like a creative suite, not a config page.

  • Brainstorm → UAT → Review → Patch pipeline
  • Feature queues with Yes / No / Later / Expand decisions
  • Demos that span games, tools, and docs
Explore AtlasStudio

Atlas-V Engine

A routing brain that thinks in orbits: Navigator, Memory, Guard, and FlowSim. Designed for systems that juggle decisions and time.

  • Explainable route diagrams and console-style logs
  • Intent, constraints, and outcomes shown visually
  • Blueprints for patchable flows, not a live backend
See the Atlas-V model

Octopus in Action

A cozy harbor-town sim with a gentleman octopus mayor. This is the flagship world used to prove the stack in practice.

  • Mailbox-driven civic tasks and city hall decisions
  • Day/night rhythms and gentle schedule pressure
  • Design playground for Atlas-V and AtlasStudio ideas
Visit the harbor

Simulation-first everything.

The same mental model that powers a harbor-town sim can sketch out operations dashboards, routing tools, and guided knowledge flows. Change the art and constraints; keep the simulation brain.

Games & sims

Management games, city-sims, training worlds, or explorable “what-if” sandboxes. AtlasForge focuses on systems depth and readable decisions.

Ops & tools

Internal dashboards, timelines, and routing logic that borrow ideas from game design: clear feedback, visible trade-offs, and narrative-style explanations.

Knowledge & docs

Guided experiences that turn static knowledge into navigable stories: branching flows, decision logs, and scenario-driven walkthroughs.
