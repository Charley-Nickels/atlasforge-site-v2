# AtlasForge Brand Canon (Phase 4F)

## Components
- AtlasForge Interactive (umbrella; home at `/index.html`)
- AtlasStudio Workstation (short: AtlasStudio)
- Atlas-V Engine
- Octopus in Action (world page only)
- S/K/B (audio label)

## Color & Motifs
- AtlasForge: magenta rings
- AtlasStudio: ember shards
- Atlas-V: cyan lattice
- OIA: warm coastal oranges
- S/K/B: violet nebula
- Elevated neutrals: `--surface-850`, `--surface-750`, and `--border-strong` for high-contrast panels while keeping balanced lighting.

## Lighting Policy
- Balanced, subtle accent lighting only; single or dual accent colors.
- No rainbow/RGB/underglow/neon.

## Typography & Layout
- Shared grid: 4–8–12–16 spacing rhythm.
- Rounded geometry; dark backgrounds; clear hierarchy (H1–H4).
- Cards with soft shadows; primary/ghost buttons; chips for tags.

## Interaction States
- Idle neutral; hover slight scale + accent highlight; active brief compression; loading soft pulse; error calm red.

## Accessibility
- Landmark roles, focus-visible outlines, ARIA for nav/toggles, keyboard-first support.

## Pages
- `/index.html` umbrella home.
- `/atlasstudio.html` workstation overview.
- `/atlasv.html` engine modules and diagrams.
- `/oia.html` world page (no tool language).
- `/skb.html` label page (no AI/tool language).
- `/games.html` showcase; `/about.html` mission/values.

## Privacy
- All pages include `<meta name="robots" content="noindex,nofollow">` until launch.
