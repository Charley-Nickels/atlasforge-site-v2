About AtlasForge Interactive

AtlasForge is a small studio focused on building tools, simulations, and games that feel handcrafted but powered by smart systems.

What we’re trying to do

The long-term goal is a unified engine and workstation that can support games, web tools, internal dashboards, data pipelines, and future interactive formats — all built with a simulation-first mindset.

  • Atlas-V as the engine / project brain.
  • AtlasStudio as the workstation / UI.
  • Octopus in Action and other worlds as live testbeds.
  • S/K/B as the audio and signal lab.

Why everything is “visual only” for now

Every demo on this site is front-end only. That’s deliberate: it lets us explore how the engine and workstation should feel, without implying that a finished SaaS product already exists.

As we harden the ideas and pipelines, the intent is to invite a small set of collaborators to shape which pieces become API surfaces, products, or internal tools.
