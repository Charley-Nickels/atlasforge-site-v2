About the Studio

AtlasForge Interactive

AtlasForge Interactive is a small studio focused on building tools, simulations, and games that feel handcrafted but powered by smart systems. We treat every project as part of a connected ecosystem: the Atlas-V engine, the AtlasStudio workstation, and the worlds that live on top.

Our Mission

AtlasForge exists to lower the friction between ideas and working prototypes. We combine strong production values with automation, simulation, and AI-assisted workflows so that concepts can move from sketch to live build quickly — without losing craft.

The long-term goal is a unified engine and workstation that can support games, web tools, internal dashboards, data pipelines, and future interactive formats.

What We Build

The AtlasForge portfolio is split into three core tracks:

  • Atlas-V — a logic and content engine for simulations and tools.
  • AtlasStudio — the workstation where those experiences are assembled.
  • Octopus in Action and other IP — the playable worlds that prove the stack.

Systems First

We think in systems: routing, scheduling, automation, and simulation. AtlasForge projects are designed to scale from small prototypes to larger worlds without rewriting everything from scratch.

Tools for Builders

AtlasStudio is being built as an internal workstation first — a place where we can connect AI, design, code, audio, and testing into a single repeatable flow before opening it up more broadly.

Flagship Worlds

Octopus in Action is our flagship IP: a cozy, systems-heavy world featuring Mayor Octavius and a town full of characters. It serves as both a game and a testbed for Atlas-V and AtlasStudio.
