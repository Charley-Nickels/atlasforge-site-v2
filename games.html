Games & labs

The AtlasForge lineup.

Cozy sims, automation worlds, RTS and tabletop experiments—all prototyped in AtlasStudio and guided by Atlas-V. Everything here is currently a visual roadmap, but the intent is to ship.

Flagship · TIDEGLASS-01

Octopus in Action

Harbor-town civic sim with animal neighbors, mailbox tasks, and city hall decisions. Built to feel cozy, but with systems deep enough to stress-test the engine and workstation.

  • City hall choices that ripple through the town.
  • Mailbox tasks as a visible to-do surface.
  • NPC schedules, jobs, and relationships.
  • Integration concepts for Atlas-V and AtlasStudio.
