Flagship world

Octopus in Action

A harbor-town civic sim used as a playful surface for AtlasForge. Think neighbor drama, mailbox tasks, city hall decisions, and quiet systems under the hood.

A vignette inside the Atlas stack

Octopus in Action is both a game concept and a testbed: everything we learn about simulation, pacing, and tools feeds back into Atlas-V and AtlasStudio.

  • Mayor Octavius and civic choices that ripple through town.
  • Mailbox and tasks as a visible to-do system.
  • NPC schedules, housing, and jobs as simulation targets.
  • Hooks for engine and workstation integration.
