Audio Lab

S/K/B builds sound palettes for worlds and flows.

The lab blends ambient beds, loops, stems, and alternate cuts — all structured the same way AtlasForge structures work: tasks, routes, versions, and mood state.

Each world has its own audio identity. OIA has harbor-night pads, market bustle loops, hill-route winds, and late ferry synths. S/K/B shapes these into packs you can browse and preview.
