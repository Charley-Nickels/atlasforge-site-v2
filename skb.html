S/K/B · Audio Lab Scores, textures, and experiments for the Atlas stack

Soundtracks for worlds, tools, and strange in-between spaces.

S/K/B is the audio arm of AtlasForge Interactive: a space for building soundtracks, ambient layers, and experimental cues for games like Octopus in Action and future simulations.

See games in development Read about OIA’s world

This page is a hub for the audio direction and philosophy. Links to releases or playlists can be added later as projects solidify.

Audio direction

Warm, human, a little haunted.

The S/K/B sound leans into warmth and texture. Even when the stack is futuristic, the music leans human — small rooms, imperfect recordings, and intentional space.

For Octopus in Action

Gentle harbor themes with subtle unease: life is cozy, but decisions still matter.

  • Soft piano + pads for mornings
  • Light percussion for dock work
  • Reflective, lo-fi nights on the pier

For tools & dashboards

Low-distraction ambient beds for long work sessions inside AtlasStudio-style environments.

  • Slow-moving chords & textures
  • No jump-scare transitions
  • Designed for focus, not hype

Palette

Mood bands instead of genres.

Instead of locking into one genre per project, S/K/B is organized by mood bands that can travel between worlds.

  • Harbor Calm: warm, slightly worn sounds for cozy, everyday scenes.
  • Decision Tension: subtle pulses and drones under big choices, without going full “boss music.”
  • Ops Focus: neutral but supportive beds for working in AtlasStudio or similar tools.

Future releases

As projects mature, this page will link to:

  • OIA soundtrack samplers
  • Focus mixes for workstation use
  • Standalone experimental tracks
S/K/B exists to keep the stack from feeling sterile. Even the most technical tools deserve good music.
